Who are we

Kung Fu Warriors is an Asian Kung Fu / Martial Arts / Culture inspired NFT living on the Solana block chain. Our goal is to promote fitness & wellbeing as well as to showcase our love of Asian cultural exports

What is "Kung Fu"?

The term "Kung Fu" is made up of two characters. The first, “Kung”, means discipline, hard work, perseverance, or endeavor. The second, “Fu”, means man or is used as a nominal suffix with diverse meanings. Combined, “Kung Fu” can refer to any discipline or skills achieved through hard work and practice, emphasizing strength, balance, endurance and peace.

Philosophy.

We believe that Martial Arts/ Kung Fu is not only a beautiful testament to our cultural heritage, but also embodies a spirit of strength and balance that can resonate universally with our next generation audience, regardless of background and culture. This collection is a reminder that beneath the surface, we are not so different after all.

Goals.

Through our collection, we hope to promote the tradition and spirit embodied by Martial Arts/ sports, encourage healthy living and fitness, and facilitate a diversified cultural exchange for all Kung Fu and NFT art lovers.

Aesthetic Art.

Every attribute of our Warriors has been hand-drawn by our Artist from scratch after countless tweaks. These attributes are inspired by classic Martial Arts, iconic movies and modern culture. We are laser focused on perfecting every detail to bring you the highest quality art that you love.
