Weekly Game Nights

In order to bring our community together, we are hosting different games on a weekly basis with SOL & NFT Prizes

Winner gets a NFT and a "Game Master Role” ( Note: These special roles make you eligible for our monthly lucky draw of 1 SOL! ). Games played include: Poker Night, Gartic Games (Pictionary), LOLBeans.io and more

KFW Youtube

We launched a Youtube channel, to showcase our full length fitness content, interviews, and more. KFW - It’s Art!

Partnerships and benefits

Various benefits and discounts from our partnership organisations, with a focus on well-being and all-round health. We actively seek to provide interesting and unique opportunities for our Warriors to try.

From whitelist partnerships with other NFT projects to restaurants and exclusive membership clubs, we have a whole range of benefits planned for our holders.

Visit our Discord to stay up to date on our latest holder benefits.

Giving Back

8% of our net mint proceeds will be donated to charity(ies) of our community's choice. Every Warrior would contribute to making the world a better place.

Based on our community’s choice, our donations will be split evenly between Save the Children, Foodlink Foundation, and Impact HK

Staking, Utilities, Games

We're currently developing a staking mechanism with our own KFW Tokens & multiple Utilities, such as: recruiting (Redeeming) NFTs directly, drawing random KFW NFT from a pool, buying future KFW Merch & our partnership products, as well as to be used in our P2E Game in Phase 2

Stay tuned for more information!
