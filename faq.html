Non-Fungible Tokens are unique, easily verifiable digital assets that can represent items such as GIFs, images, videos, music albums, and more. Anything that exists online can be purchased as an NFT, theoretically.

NFTs are different from cryptocurrencies because they’re not interchangeable. Think of Pokémon cards: You can trade them, but a Gastly is not the same as a holographic Charizard. But a bitcoin is indistinguishable from another bitcoin.

How do I buy SOL (Solana)?

SOL (Solana) is a cryptocurrency, and this project is on the Solana Blockchain.

If you are new to the crypto space we recommend that you sign up to Binance.com on your desktop or download the phone app.

  1. You will need to buy Solana first using your local currency (USD, HKD, GBP etc)
  2. Click on "Buy Now" on the home page
  3. Select your local currency (in the example below I selected USD) on "Spend" and select "USDT" for "Receive". Select the amount you want to spend on our NFT. Then click continue.
  4. Once you have USDT, click on "convert"
  5. Here, you will need to convert your USDT to Solana (SOL) → click "Preview Conversion" and proceed.
  6. Congratulations, you now have Solana (SOL) to buy your first NFT!
Here's how to send Solana from your Binance wallet to your Phantom wallet:
  1. Create a Phantom wallet https://phantom.app and add it to your Chrome as an extension (Phantom wallet is a Solana wallet, it is only available on your desktop for now, they will launch on mobile early next year - now you know how early you are in this space!)
  2. In your Phantom wallet, you can click on the long number at the top (which is your unique ID of your wallet) and it will automatically copy the number
  3. Go back on your Binance, click on "Wallet" → "Overview" → "Withdraw" →
    1. Select coin, select "SOL"
    2. Address, make sure to paste your Phantom wallet address here (double check)
    3. Network, make sure to select "SOL" as the network
    4. Select the amount of SOL you want to withdraw and then click withdraw
  4. Give it a few seconds/ sometimes minutes, and check your Phantom wallet. Your SOL should be available on your Phantom wallet now!
  5. Go on our official website. Click "Connect wallet" when the mint is opened, then click "Mint Now", You will be prompted to approve the transaction - after approval, your NFT will show up in your wallet in seconds! Remember, always use a "Burner" wallet to mint and double check the mint site. We will never DM you links!!
